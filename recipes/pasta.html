Simple Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Description:

Tender pasta tossed in a smooth tomato sauce with garlic and olive oil, creating a light, flavorful, and easy everyday meal.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water
  2. Fry garlic in oil
  3. Add tomato sauce + salt
  4. Mix with pasta
